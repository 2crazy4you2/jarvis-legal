Jarvis is a content management tool for creators. Upload once, publish to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook with platform-specific captions, scheduling, and full control over your post settings.

Features

Multi-platform publishing

Publish a single video to multiple platforms simultaneously, each with its own caption, privacy settings, and interaction controls.

Scheduling

Schedule posts for the right time on each platform. A calendar view shows all upcoming content across every connected account.

Creator controls

Set privacy levels, enable or disable comments, duets, and stitches, and manage commercial content disclosure before publishing.

TikTok YouTube Instagram Facebook
