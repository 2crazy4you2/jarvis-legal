What Jarvis Does

Multi-Platform Publishing

Upload a video once and publish it to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook simultaneously. Each platform gets its own optimized caption and settings.

Scheduling

Schedule posts for the optimal time on each platform. View all upcoming posts in a unified calendar view across all connected accounts.

Creator Controls

Full control over privacy settings, interaction permissions, and commercial content disclosure. All metadata is editable before publishing.
