Jarvis by CrazNeax — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

What data we collect

Jarvis collects analytics data from the app owner's own TikTok account, including follower count, video views, and video list via the TikTok Display API. It also facilitates uploading and scheduling video content to the owner's TikTok profile via the Content Posting API. No data from any other user is collected at any time.

How we use the data

Analytics data is used solely to display personal growth metrics in a private local dashboard. Video content is uploaded exclusively to the owner's own TikTok profile on the owner's instruction. No data is ever shared, sold, or transmitted to any third party.

Data storage

All analytics data is stored locally on the owner's personal device in a private SQLite database. Video files are cached temporarily during the upload process and deleted immediately after uploading is complete. No data is stored on external servers.

Third-party services

This app uses the TikTok Login Kit, Display API, and Content Posting API in accordance with TikTok's Platform Terms of Service and Developer Policies. Access tokens are stored locally and securely on the owner's device and are never shared.

Content posting

Video content uploaded through Jarvis is posted exclusively to the owner's own TikTok account on the owner's direct instruction. Jarvis does not post content autonomously, does not post to any other user's account, and does not retain uploaded video files after the upload process is complete.

User rights

As this app is solely for personal use, no external users have accounts or data stored within this application.
